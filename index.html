What is Skin Cycling?

Skin cycling was created by Dr. Whitney Bowe and it is a strategic method for cycling through your nightly skincare products to drive the best results. This was created with skin barrier optimization in mind and it encourages you to use products in a strategic way to compliment one another.

Skin cycling involves a 4 night cycle which includes Exfoliation Night, Retinoid Night and Recovery Nights.
